Design Principles

Signal Over Noise

Every element must justify its existence. Remove anything that doesn't help understand the data.

Density Over Whitespace

Operational users scan for patterns. Show more data with tighter, purposeful spacing.

Semantic Color

Color indicates meaning (status, category, threshold) — never decoration.

Dark Mode First

Infrastructure teams work in terminals and dark UIs. Design natively for dark backgrounds.

Purposeful Motion

Transitions communicate state changes, not style. Motion must reinforce stability.

Accessible Always

Keyboard navigation, ARIA labels, sufficient contrast, non-color signaling.
